UniFit Health is an AI-powered health and wellness tracker designed specifically for college students. It brings water tracking, calorie counting, steps monitoring, gym logging, posture analysis and personalized health insights into one simple and beautiful app. The project uses **Gemini 3 Pro multimodal AI**, built with the help of **Google AI Studio**, to analyze food photos, body posture images and daily activity to deliver clear, meaningful recommendations.

This project is deployed seamlessly on **Vercel** for fast, reliable, production-ready performance.

---

##  Features

### **Welcome & Onboarding**
Users log in with Google and answer basic questions about their diet, weight goals, routine and lifestyle.  
Gemini generates a personalized health plan.

### **Dashboard**
A clean overview of daily steps, water intake, calories in/out, gym minutes and AI health summary.

### **MealScan**
Upload a food photo and get calories, macros, health rating and personalized advice powered by Gemini 3 Pro Vision.

### **HydraAI**
Track water intake using quick buttons or custom input.  
Smart hydration recommendations automatically adapt to user activity.

### **PainScan**
Upload a posture or body photo to detect possible muscle strain, pain severity and recovery tips.

### **GymTrack & WorkoutHistory**
Log workouts and review progress and consistency over time.

### **CalorieHistory & HydrationHistory**
Visual graphs showing daily and weekly calorie and hydration trends.

### **StepsHistory**
Simple chart showing step patterns throughout the week.

### **ProfileSettings**
Update personal details, goals and preferences anytime.
